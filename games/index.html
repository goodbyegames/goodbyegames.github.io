Freddie’s hard drive has crashed, losing all his old memories with it, but Adieu Lost and Found Interactive Explorer (A.L.F.I.E.) will help Freddie get them back. “Some Goodbyes We Made” is a minigame collection about goodbyes, old memories, and letting go. Through 15 minigames with special mechanics, remember, let go, and practice how to say goodbye.
